Our Story

Baked Small. Delivered Local.

The Harley Co. started with one oven, one neighborhood, and a simple idea: bake a small, honest menu every week and get it to people fresh — no warehouses, no shelf life, just good baking on a short route.

We're based in Saint Johns, FL, and every item is made in small batches so it's baked close to the day it reaches you. The menu changes with the season and whatever's worth getting excited about that week.
